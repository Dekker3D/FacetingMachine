# Off-the-Shelf Components - Gem Faceting Machine

## Linear Rails
### MGN9H Linear Rail System
**Manufacturer**: Various (AliExpress, industrial suppliers)
**Application**: Vertical height adjustment system

**Rail Specifications:**
- **Width**: 9mm
- **Height**: 6.5mm
- **Length**: Custom (based on travel requirements)
- **Material**: Steel hardened and ground

**Carriage Specifications:**
- **Width**: 20mm
- **Length**: 39.9mm
- **Height**: 8mm
- **Overhang**: 10mm from rail surface
- **Clearance below**: 2mm
- **Ball diameter**: 3mm (standard)

**Mounting:**
- **Hole pattern**: 15mm spacing (left/right), 16mm spacing (length)
- **Fasteners**: M3x8 bolts (estimated)
- **Mounting surface**: Flat, clean mounting required

**Configuration**: Two carriages on single rail for maximum stiffness

## Leadscrew System
### T8 Leadscrew (Simple Thread Type)
**Manufacturer**: AliExpress suppliers
**Note**: Marketed as "T8" but actually simple screw thread, not ball screw

**Screw Specifications:**
- **Type**: Simple screw thread
- **Diameter**: 8mm
- **Pitch**: 2mm
- **Length**: 300mm
- **Material**: Steel

**Nut Specifications:**
- **Type**: Standard nut (no internal threading for mounting holes)
- **Diameter**: 22mm
- **Thickness**: 3.5mm
- **Flange diameter**: 22mm
- **Mounting holes**: 4x ø3.5mm
- **Hole spacing**: 8mm radius from center
- **Mounting method**: Wood screws into 3D printed part
- **Note**: No internal threading in mounting holes

## Aluminum Extrusion
### 20x20mm T-Slot Extrusion
**Application**: Main structural mast replacement
**Material**: 6061 Aluminum (recommended)

**Specifications:**
- **Size**: 20mm × 20mm square profile
- **T-slot**: 10mm wide × 6mm deep
- **T-slot position**: ±2mm from center (top and bottom faces)
- **Length**: Custom cut to requirements

## Bearings
### Leadscrew Bearings
- **Type**: Standard deep groove ball bearings
- **Inner diameter**: 8mm (matches leadscrew)
- **Outer diameter**: To be determined based on availability
- **Width**: Standard (typically 7-10mm)
- **Application**: Supporting leadscrew at both ends

### Hinge Bearings
- **Type**: Deep groove ball bearings
- **Outer diameter**: 15mm (for quill assembly interface)
- **Inner diameter**: 8mm (assumed, to be verified)
- **Width**: 7mm (assumed)
- **Application**: Quill assembly hinge system

### Quill Assembly Interface
- **Current bushing**: 15mm length with 0.5mm tolerance on 14.5mm mast
- **Interface**: Bearings with 15mm OD to fit existing quill bushing
- **Material**: Steel or stainless steel

## Fasteners
### M3 Hardware
- **Bolts**: M3x8 (estimated for rail mounting)
- **Nuts**: M3 nuts
- **Washers**: M3 washers
- **Material**: Stainless steel A2/A4 for corrosion resistance

### Wood Screws
- **Size**: #4 or #6 (for nut mounting)
- **Length**: 10-15mm
- **Application**: Securing leadscrew nut to 3D printed part
